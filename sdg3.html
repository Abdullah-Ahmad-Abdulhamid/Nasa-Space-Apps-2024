Key Targets

Global Health Objectives

🤱

Reduce the global maternal mortality ratio to less than 70 per 100,000 live births.

🦠

End the epidemics of AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria and neglected tropical diseases.

🏥

Achieve universal health coverage, including financial risk protection and access to quality care.

💊

Support the research and development of vaccines and medicines for communicable diseases.

🚭

Strengthen the implementation of the WHO Framework Convention on Tobacco Control.

🚑

Substantially increase health financing and the recruitment of the health workforce.

Why It Matters

The Heart of Development

Health is a human right. It is a key component of sustainable development. By ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being, we can improve productivity and the quality of life for everyone. Healthy people are the foundation for healthy economies. Investing in health prevents families from falling back into poverty.
