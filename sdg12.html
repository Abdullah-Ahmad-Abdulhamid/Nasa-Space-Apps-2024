Key Targets

What SDG 12 Aims to Achieve

📋

Implement the 10-Year Framework of Programmes on sustainable consumption and production.

🏢

Encourage companies, especially large and transnational companies, to adopt sustainable practices.

🍎

Reduce by half per capita global food waste at the retail and consumer levels.

Why It Matters

Preserving Our Future Lifeline

Sustainable consumption and production are essential to sustain the livelihoods of current and future generations. It is key to minimizing natural resource use and the impacts on the environment while simultaneously fostering economic growth.
