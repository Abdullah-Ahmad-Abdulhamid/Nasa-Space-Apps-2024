Key Targets

What SDG 15 Aims to Achieve

⛰️

Ensure the conservation of mountain ecosystems and their vast biodiversity.

🏜️

Combat desertification and restore degraded land to reach land degradation neutrality.

🐆

Take urgent action to protect and prevent the extinction of threatened species.

Why It Matters

Ecosystems Sustain Us

Healthy ecosystems are vital for human survival and well-being. They provide essential services, such as clean air and water, climate regulation, and fertile soil for agriculture. Protecting biodiversity ensures that we can sustain these services for generations to come. Deforestation and land degradation undermine our food security and accelerate climate change.
