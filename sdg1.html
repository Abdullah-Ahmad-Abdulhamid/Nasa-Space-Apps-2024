Key Targets

What SDG 1 Aims to Achieve

🚫

End extreme poverty for all people everywhere, in all its forms.

📉

Reduce by half the number of people living in poverty across all dimensions.

🛡️

Implement nationally appropriate social protection systems and measures for all.

🏥

Ensure equal access to education, healthcare, and economic resources.

🌍

Build resilience of the poor to climate-related events and economic shocks.

🤝

Mobilize significant resources from all sources to implement poverty-ending programs.

Why It Matters

The Foundation of All Goals

Poverty is not just about a lack of income — it encompasses a lack of access to education, healthcare, clean water, and economic opportunity. It creates a cycle that is difficult to break without deliberate, coordinated action. By ending poverty, we create the foundation for economic growth, social stability, and sustainable development across all 17 Global Goals. Every goal — from zero hunger to quality education — depends on lifting people out of poverty first.
